Game Features

Bomb Transfer

The bomb is in your hands and the timer is already running. Your only way out is to chase someone down and collide into them before it hits zero. No resets, no mercy. The long you hold it, the worse it gets.

Body-Part Scoring

Not all tags are equal. A clean tag in the head is worth 100 points, but sloppy tags to the legs only get you 30. If you want to top the leaderboard, you ned to be smart about where you land your hits.

No Eliminations

Fallinf off the island does not save you from the chaos. You respawn immediately and jump right back into the fight, but you lose 50 points every tuime you go down. Five full minutes, all four player, no way out until the clock hits zero.

Sets in the Sky

A floating island with no walls and open edges on every side. There is nowhere to hide and noweher to run for long. The tight space keeps everyone dangerously close at all times, and one wrong steps send you over the edge.
