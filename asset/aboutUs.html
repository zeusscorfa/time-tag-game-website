About Darkon 5

We are a small group of student developers who share a strong interest in creating games that people can enjoy with their friends. What started as a school project turned into a passion for building interactive experiences that are simple to pick up but still exciting to play. We focus on ideas that bring players together, whether through competition, teamwork, or just having fun in unpredictable moments.

As students, we are still learning and experimenting with different styles, mechanics, and technologies. Every project we create is part of our growth, allowing us to improve our skills while exploring new concepts in game design. We value creativity, collaboration, and feedback, and we take each challenge as an opportunity to get better.

Our goal is to continue developing games that reflect our ideas and personality as a team. We may be starting small, but we are committed to creating experiences that players will remember and enjoy.
